2017 - 2019 Summary

Ecobot Competition

Second-year competition showcase, Feb 2019

Competed in the school-wide engineering competition with the theme of designing an eco-friendly robot that could differentiate organic/inorganic wastes. With a group of 6 team members, the group implemented a waste collecting robot to differentiate between grapes and marbles. My tasks was to design and prototype the mechanical assembly of the collecting and sorting mechanisms on the robot, and then refined the design through iterative testing process. After the competition, the team won second place and the robot also received the best people's award.

Human-powered Light Generator

First-year project, April 2018

Design and prototype a light generator by storing human kinetic energy to the capacitor for areas without electricity. Tasks include generating design concept, creating initial design using cardbaords, selecting components and materials, and testing the device power efficiency.
